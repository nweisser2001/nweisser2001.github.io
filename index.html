Nicolette Weisser

ABOUT

Musical Theatre Performer - Vocalist - Pianist

Orlando-based multi-instrumentalist and musical theatre performer, Nicolette Weisser seeks to transport those, within their respective audiences, to a place outside of the tedious and exhausting realities of day-to-day life and to a place of wonder, imagination, and possibilities through the outlet of theatrical art. Nicolette has been a high-achieving student throughout her academic career (top 15% of high school class, national honor society, etc.) and expects to add to these achievements with her graduation from Berklee College of Music. Most recently, she appeared in the Dr. Phillips Center for the Performing Arts production of “A Funny Thing Happened on the Way to the Forum” and “Queen of the People’s Hearts” presented by the Bechdel Theatre Group.

Following her graduation, Nicolette will be the founder and CEO of Nicolette Weisser Music, a multi-faceted performing arts business that strives to inspire and ignite the creative spark within audiences young and old by providing performance in piano, acting, and singing within a professional theatre setting as well as utilizing the skills within those areas to coach the performers of tomorrow.

Nicolette Weisser Music is founded on the creation of exceptional interpersonal relationships rooted in honesty and trust that is then directly translated into the creation of authentic and meaningful art that emphasizes the need for performers of all different background, training, culture, ethnicities, etc. to feel confident, safe, and proud of the individuality they bring to the stage with the hopes of creating a more equitable and safe theatrical performance industry.

More About Nicolette!

Nicolette currently resides in Boston, MA where she will graduate from Berklee College of Music in Spring of 2022. She frequently visits her family in the sunny and beautiful Orlando, FL only minutes away from Disney World. Nicolette is the proud mom to 15 beautiful succulent plants and 2 dogs, an English Bulldog, Luna, and Pug, Peanut. In addition to being a hard-working musician, Nicolette thoroughly enjoys reading, watching historical docuseries and British period pieces, jamming out to Taylor Swift, and adventuring around Boston and petting all of the dogs in sight.
